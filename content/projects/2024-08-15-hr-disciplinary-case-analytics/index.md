## The Challenge

Indonesia Tobacco Company needed a comprehensive HR analytics solution to track and analyze disciplinary cases across their organization. The existing manual tracking system made it difficult to identify patterns, monitor trends, and make data-driven HR decisions.

## The Solution

Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard with advanced DAX calculations:

**Dashboard Features:**
- Real-time tracking of disciplinary cases by function, grade, and tenure
- Trend analysis showing case patterns over time
- Comparative analytics across departments
- Drill-down capabilities for detailed case investigation

**Technical Implementation:**
- Built complex DAX measures for HR metrics
- Created dynamic filters for multi-dimensional analysis
- Implemented role-based access control
- Designed intuitive visualizations for non-technical stakeholders

## Results & Impact

- Enabled data-driven HR decision making
- Reduced case resolution time by identifying bottlenecks
- Provided insights into organizational behavior patterns
- Improved transparency in disciplinary processes
- Facilitated proactive HR interventions
