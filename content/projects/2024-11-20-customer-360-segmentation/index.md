## The Challenge

Bank Jago needed to understand their 10 million user base better and reduce customer churn. The challenge was to segment customers effectively and predict which customers were at risk of churning, enabling targeted retention campaigns.

## The Solution

Built a comprehensive customer analytics system using RFM segmentation and machine learning:

**RFM Segmentation:**
- Analyzed Recency, Frequency, and Monetary value for all customers
- Created customer segments: Champions, Loyal, At-Risk, Lost
- Enabled personalized marketing strategies for each segment

**Churn Prediction Model:**
- Developed machine learning model to predict churn propensity
- Analyzed customer behavior patterns and transaction history
- Identified key churn indicators and risk factors

**Technical Stack:**
- Python for data processing and modeling
- BigQuery for large-scale data analysis
- SQL for data extraction and transformation
- Visualization dashboards for stakeholder communication

## Results & Impact

- Segmented 10 million users into actionable customer groups
- Achieved 85% accuracy in churn prediction
- Enabled targeted retention campaigns for at-risk customers
- Increased customer lifetime value through personalized engagement
- Reduced churn rate by identifying early warning signals
